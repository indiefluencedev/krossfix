Who We Are

VCube Chemicals, a renowned name in the chemical industry with a legacy of excellence, proudly presents KrossFix. Based in Rewari, Haryana, VCube Chemicals has been at the forefront of producing premium products such as natural gum, resin, photographic chemicals, and lignosulphate. With a history of innovation and quality, our parent company has earned a stellar reputation. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, located in Nadiad GIDC near Ahmedabad, upholds the highest standards of quality and sustainability. Leveraging this extensive expertise, KrossFix is dedicated to providing advanced adhesive solutions that set new benchmarks in India's footwear industry.

Solvent-Based Products

This range includes cleaners, primers, adhesives, and hardeners. Designed for high-strength bonding, our solvent-based products offer exceptional durability and ease of use for various footwear applications.

Water-Based Primers and Adhesives

Our water-based primers and adhesives are available in both 1-component and 2-component solutions, providing a safe and eco-friendly alternative with excellent bonding properties.

Hotmelt Adhesives

Our PUMELT adhesive technology offers a sustainable solution to replace traditional solvent-based adhesives. These hotmelt adhesives ensure strong, flexible bonds and increased efficiency in the manufacturing process.

BTX-Free Adhesives

Prioritizing safety and sustainability, our BTX-free solvent-based cleaners, primers, and adhesives are free from harmful benzene, toluene, and xylene, ensuring a safer work environment without compromising on performance.

Cyanoacrylate Adhesives

Ideal for quick repairs and precise applications, our cyanoacrylate adhesives provide instant bonding and exceptional strength for various shoe components.
